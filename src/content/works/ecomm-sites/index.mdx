E-commerce platforms I have worked on include Guitar Village and One Stop Grow
Shop. These projects involved managing client relationships, overseeing
development, and ensuring the smooth operation of the e-commerce sites.

## Guitar Village

Managed client relationship and e-commerce platform at Guitar Village using Ruby on Rails.

Integrated various payment gateways including Stripe and Sage Pay to provide a
seamless checkout experience for customers.

Provided ongoing maintenance and updates to the platform, ensuring it remained
secure, up-to-date, and optimized for performance. Provided technical support
and training to the client's team, enabling them to manage the platform
effectively.

[Link to guitarvillage.co.uk](https://guitarvillage.co.uk/)

## One Stop Grow Shop

Managed client relationship and e-commerce platform at One Stop Grow Shop
using Spree Commerce (Ruby on Rails).

Integrated various payment gateways including Stripe, Checkout.com, and crypto
payment options to provide a seamless checkout experience for customers.

Provided ongoing maintenance and updates to the platform, ensuring it remained
secure, up-to-date, and optimized for performance.

Provided technical support and training to the client's team, enabling them to
manage the platform effectively.

[Link to onestopgrowshop.co.uk](https://www.onestopgrowshop.co.uk)

## Other Platforms

I have also worked on e-commerce platforms using Shopify and WooCommerce,
providing similar services including client management, platform development,
payment gateway integration, and ongoing support.

## Payment Gateway Integrations

I have experience integrating a variety of payment gateways into e-commerce
platforms, including:

- Stripe
- Checkout.com
- Sage Pay
- Crypto Payment Options
