E-commerce platforms I have worked on include **Guitar Village** and **One Stop Grow Shop**. These projects involved managing client relationships, overseeing development, and ensuring the smooth operation of the e-commerce sites.

## Guitar Village

Managed client relationship and e-commerce platform at Guitar Village using **Ruby on Rails**.

Integrated various payment gateways including **Stripe** and **Sage Pay** to provide a seamless checkout experience for customers.

Provided ongoing maintenance and updates to the platform, ensuring it remained secure, up-to-date, and optimized for performance.
Provided technical support and training to the client's team, enabling them to manage the platform effectively.

[Link to guitarvillage.co.uk](https://guitarvillage.co.uk/)


## One Stop Grow Shop

Managed client relationship and e-commerce platform at One Stop Grow Shop using **Spree Commerce** (Ruby on Rails).

Integrated various payment gateways including **Stripe**, **Checkout.com**, and **crypto payment options** to provide a seamless checkout experience for customers.

Provided ongoing maintenance and updates to the platform, ensuring it remained secure, up-to-date, and optimized for performance.

Provided technical support and training to the client's team, enabling them to manage the platform effectively.

[Link to onestopgrowshop.co.uk](https://www.onestopgrowshop.co.uk)
