## Innovative fintech focused on making everyday transactions smarter

At Streeva, I enhanced the Gift Aid platform Swiftaid—used by JustGiving,
Shelter, and Comic Relief—by moving the codebase to TypeScript, aligning Vue
apps with C# services, and introducing BDD to boost reliability. I also
delivered secure portals, including an HMRC proof of concept with
Elasticsearch and Kibana, while improving APIs, security, and deployment to
support charities in raising more through Gift Aid.
