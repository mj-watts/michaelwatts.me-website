## Launched crossâ€‘platform music streaming app with collaborative party mode

I helped develop IO Music, a cross-platform app built with React Native, native iOS
components, and Firebase backend services. Its standout party mode let users
create shared playlists and control playback collaboratively in real time,
powered by WebSockets for low-latency sync.

Alongside standard streaming features like recommendations, offline playback,
and social sharing, the app reached the top 10 in the Apple charts and gained
100,000+ downloads in its first month, earning positive reviews for both its
innovative functionality and user-friendly design.
